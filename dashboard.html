Most useful business actions

  • Prepare staffing and inventory for Saturdays and the 7 PM peak.
  • Use January sales performance as the short-term benchmark.
  • Keep Food and beverages highly visible because it leads revenue.
  • Localize branch promotions: Branch C favors Food and beverages, Branch A favors Home and lifestyle, and Branch B is strong in Sports and travel plus Health and beauty.
  • Analyze customer satisfaction separately from basket value because rating and sales are not meaningfully related.
